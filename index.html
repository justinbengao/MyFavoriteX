Story

Solo Leveling is a Korean web novel that was adapted into a webtoon and anime. It follows the journey of Sung Jinwoo, the weakest of all hunters in a world where dungeons and monsters exist. As a "E-rank" hunter, he faces death every day while being tasked with low-level dungeon hunts. However, one fateful event changes his life, granting him the ability to level up and grow stronger without limit.
